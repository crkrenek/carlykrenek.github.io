Mortar & Pestle | July-August 2021

Description

The client, Mortar & Pestle, requested an initial website design for their Vermont based cafe/bakery.

My Roles

  • Research
  • User Personas
  • Use Case & Storyboard
  • Wireframe
  • UX/UI Design Mockup

Tools Used

  • Adobe Photoshop

The Challenge

Design a new website for the cafe/bakery, Mortar & Pestle, that will help attract new customers and increase sales by offering an online ordering experience.

Phase 1: Define & Research

In place of an initial discovery meeting, I collected information about the company, their needs, and goals via a client survey created with Google Forms. The responses I received informed my competitive analysis and allowed me to determine a user base for Mortar & Pestle.

Personas

Four distinct personas were created to build empathy with visitors of the site and identify their potential user goals and needs.

Eric Singh | 19

Undergraduate Student

Champlain College

Interaction Design Major

As an interaction design major, foodie, and avid Instagrammer, Eric appreciates a trendy aesthetic with a simplistic interface. He's looking to find quality images of the food and drinks, a menu with pricing, and images of the bakery/cafe. Eric enjoys hanging at Mortar & Pestle when working on classwork, but he also enjoys going out in the evenings for live music with friends. So, he will also be looking for event information on the website.

Rachel Harris | 28

Digital Marketing Specialist

Rachel loves a sweet treat and cup of coffee, especially when working on projects. She's also a people person and likes to treat her coworkers every once in a while. Most days Rachel is dropping in on her way to work to pick up breakfast for herself, but sometimes she likes to order coffee and goodies for the office. On occasion, she will order lunch from the office and walk over to pick it up. On the weekends, Rachel likes ot head to the cafe to catch up with friends and has been there a time or two for a date. Often times when she's using the site, she's looking to find a menu, quick online ordering, a simple catering form, and/or a rewards program or current specials.

Amy Nelson | 32

Full Time Mom

Amy visits the website ot place online orders for coffee and baked goods. She wants to be able to order through a straightforward process that is quick and easy to use. She also likes the images of the items she can show her kids when letting them pick something out online. Amy is health conscious and appreciates the fact that there are items offered on the menu that adhere to her healthy lifestyle. She likes being able to see a description of the food items that mention important nutrition information.

Mark Reed | 54

Professor

Technology

  • -Prefers to browse on his phone
  • -Uses a laptop at home or when working remotely and a desktop when working in his office on campus
  • -Comfortable using most devices, but doesn't consider himself to be tech savvy
  • -Online for a few hours a day for work and research purposes

Goals/Habits/Interests

Professor Reed often picks up coffee on his way to class, but he will also frequent the cafe with his laptop to work on grading or planning. He enjoys the art and music scene in Burlington and attends live music events at the cafe every once in awhile. When he visits the Mortar& Pestle website he is usually checking their hours of operation, live music events calendar, and their menu with pricing.

Use Cases

A use case was written for each of the unique personas in order for me to determine what tasks users will likely perform on the site and what potential alternatives routes they might take.
