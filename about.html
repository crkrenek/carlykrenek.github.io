Hello, I'm Carly Krenek.

I’m a recent Champlain College grad with a B.S. in Web Design and Development. User experience is my passion.

Coming off five years in my first profession as an educator, I’m bringing my passion for creating accessible and inclusive materials to the tech world and striving to help people on a larger scale. With admiration for technology and design, I’m committed to learning about humans and the way they interact with technology.

My love for collaboration gives me a unique ability to see things from different perspectives and empathize with others which ultimately informs my research methods and design process. You’ll find my style is rooted in simplicity and functionality with accessibility at the heart of my designs.
