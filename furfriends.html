Fur Friends Animal Shelter | Sep-Nov 2021

Description

The project covered concept, design, and development for the fictional client, Fur Friends, and was part of my Web Design & Development Practicum.

My Roles

  • Research
  • Information Architecture
  • Content Strategy & Writing
  • UI & UX Design

Tools Used

  • Figma

  • CSS
  • Javascript

The Challenge

I was tasked with creating a new website that would attract and engage visitors while offering potential adoptees, donors, and volunteers a way to support their shelter via streamlined and efficient processes online.

The client wanted users to be able to browse available animals, apply for adoption or volunteer opportunities online, as well as make online donations.

Discovery/Information Gathering

Informal Research

I reviewed and analyzed animal shelter websites with similar user needs. That process allowed me to identify elements that worked well and gave me reference points for the site’s architecture and required features. The site’s I used as inspiration for my design were San Antonio Pets Alive, Austin Humane Society, and Friends For Life.

Content Creation

I was responsible for creating the majority of the content on the site, with the exception of the Fur Friend’s logo, Adoption Application, Adoption Policy, and the Adoption FAQ. Google Drive was my organization tool of choice. I utilized Google Docs to outline written content and arrange content in folders that correspond to the sitemap I created.

Style Guide

I used Adobe Illustrator to develop a style guide to ensure that the design of Fur Friends was consistent site-wide. The document included brand voice/tone, logo usage, typography, color, grid, image, button state, and general button usage guidelines.
